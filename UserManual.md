# Synckraft CRM: User Manual

Welcome to the Synckraft CRM! This guide will help you understand how to get started and use the platform effectively.

## 1. Getting Started

### How to Sign Up
1. Go to the **Register** page.
2. Fill in your **Full Name**, **Email**, and a secure **Password**.
3. Select your intended **Role** (e.g., Sales User, Operations).
4. Click **Request Access**.

### Account Approval (PENDING Status)
After signing up, your account is placed in **PENDING** status for security. 
- You will see a screen saying **"Account Pending Approval"**.
- A **Master Admin** must verify your account and set your status to **ACTIVE** in the system before you can access the dashboard.

## 2. Master Admin Functions

If you are the **Master Admin** (`asiya@synckraft.in`), you have full control over the system.

### User Management
- Go to the **User Approvals** section in the sidebar.
- Here you can see a list of all registered users and their current status.
- You can approve new signups to give them access to the platform.

### Dashboard Overview
The **Master Dashboard** gives you a high-level view of:
- **Total Leads**: All leads captured in the system.
- **Active Leads**: Leads currently being worked on.
- **Converted**: Successful conversions.
- **Revenue & Performance**: Charts showing lead generation trends.

## 3. Daily Usage

### Navigation
- **Sidebar**: Use the sidebar on the left to navigate between different modules (Leads, Sales Team, Analytics, etc.).
- **Mobile Use**: On your phone, tap the **Menu (≡)** icon at the top left to open the sidebar. Tap the **X** or anywhere outside to close it.

### Logging Out
- Click on your profile name at the top right of the screen.
- Select **Sign Out** to securely end your session.

## 4. Tips for Success
- **Stay Active**: The session persists even if you refresh the page, so you don't have to log in every time.
- **Data Security**: Your CRM is protected by Role-Based Access Control. This means users only see the data they are authorized to see (e.g., Sales Users only see leads assigned to them).
- **Mobile Optimized**: You can safely use the CRM on your phone or tablet while on the go!
